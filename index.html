International Students' Association at SUNY Korea

The International Student's Association at SUNY Korea (ISA) is a community of International Students studying at The State University of New York, Korea, along with the students in its sister campuses in Incheon Global Campus. ISA is an university registered and recognized club at SUNY Korea since October 2nd, 2018, and it is currently the largest student club in SUNY Korea.

Every semester, ISA holds different fun activities like movie nights, cultural nights, International Dinners, etc. We welcome all international students and professors to join our club and become a part of the international family. We have a lot to do together!
